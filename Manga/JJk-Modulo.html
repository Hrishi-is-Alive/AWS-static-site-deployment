Jujutsu Kaisen Modulo

Action Fantasy

Author: Gege Akutami

Jujutsu Kaisen Modulo is a sequel series set in 2086, 68 years after Ryomen Sukuna's defeat, featuring a new, darker, and more strategic plot. It follows Yuka and Tsurugi Okkotsu (grandchildren of Yuta and Maki) investigating the abduction of humans for use as living Cursed Energy batteries, caused by the arrival of an alien race called Simurians, who are themselves jujutsu sorcerers.
